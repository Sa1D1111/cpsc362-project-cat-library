Chausie Cat

Weighing at about 30 pounds, Chausie cats are among the largest domesticated cats in existence. As a hybrid, every Chausie cat is a unique individual with a big personality to match its size. They develop strong bonds with their families and don't like to be left alone.

Key Facts

  • Size: Large
  • Coat: Short with plush undercoat and a coarser upper layer, grizzled coat colors
  • Behaviour: Energetic, Intelligent

Personality

Adopting a Chausie means taking a cat that has plenty of their wild traits, since they are half wild and half domesticated cats. Chausies need long stints during the day when they are active. They need clear spaces to run aound, climb and play with vigor. They require a high level of interaction during their playtime.
